This is a FREE event to help people of all experience levels further their learning of Python code, Mathematica code, and Circuit/Project design through interactive workshops using the Raspberry Pi micro-controller. There will also be presentations after the workshops finish, presentations will be on projects people have worked on using the Raspberry Pi. Projects will vary between research oriented projects and personal projects, the aim of the presentations is to inspire attendees to create their own unique project using what they learned during the Raspberry Jam.

The Jam will take place October 15th 2018 from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm (Time may change depending on amount of project submissions)
